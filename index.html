  • Ethos, pathos, and logos are all used to convince consumers to buy a product. An advertisement using ethos will try to build trust with the consumer. Advertisements using ethos will use the approval of professionals in the field (such as a health product saying that it is recommended by doctors) or by using testimonials from consumers. An advertisement using pathos will try to appeal to a consumerâ€™s emotions. Examples of this are advertisements invoking happiness and relating it with their product, or sadness that can be alleviated with the purchase of a product. An advertisement using logos will appeal to logic. An example of this is an advertisement providing statistics to make their product look better, such as a food item listing the nutrition facts and ingredients to make it seem healthy.
  • Advertising techniques are persuasive techniques advertisers employ to get people to buy a product. An example of an advertising technique is bandwagon. An advertisement using this technique would show many people using the product. The goal with this advertising strategy is to get the consumer to think that since everyone is using it, they should too. Another advertising technique is testimonial. An advertisement using this technique would show people giving their testimonials on the product, saying how well it worked for them and how it solved their problems. This advertising technique is meant to convince the reader that the product will do the same for them.
  • Both Advil and Tylenol are over-the-counter pain relief medications. This means that the two brands would benefit from advertisement, and thus advertise their products. In the Tylenol advertisement viewed, ethos was used when the advertisement said that doctors recommend Tylenol over any other pain relief medication. The advertisement also used pathos to connect pain-free, happy living with Tylenol. In the Advil advertisement viewed, logos was used, as the advertisement used numbers to state how long the pain relief lasted. It also used pathos to attribute Advil with being pain-free and happy.
